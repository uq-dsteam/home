AV-HARM & AuSSE Framework

AuSSE (Autonomous Vehicle Security and Safety Evaluation): A novel framework designed to assess both the cybersecurity and safety aspects of autonomous vehicles.

VHARM (Vehicle Hierarchical Attack Representation Model): The graphical security model developed within the AuSSE framework to identify attack paths and vulnerabilities.
