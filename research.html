Research Areas

Exploring cyber security modeling and analysis across diverse domains.

Research Focus: Cyber G.A.M.E

  • Graphical Models for Cyber Security: Model-based Cyber Security Risk Analysis
  • AI for Cybersecurity & Cyber Security for AI: Securing AI systems and Cybersecurity using AI techniques
  • Moving Target Defense (MTD): Resilient and Proactive Defence
  • Evolving Attacks and Defense Automation: Red team and Blue team Automation and evaluation using AI

Research Domains: CANVAS-AI

  • C – Cyber-Physical Systems: smart grids, intelligent water distribution, IoT systems
  • A – Autonomous and Unmanned Systems: UGVs, UAVs, USVs, drones, maritime vessels, autonomous vehicles
  • N – Networks: Enterprise, Cloud, Edge, Software-Defined Networking
  • V – Vehicles: Autonomous land, aerial, maritime systems
  • A – Advanced Satellite Systems
  • S – Smart Systems: integration of cyber-physical, IoT, edge computing
  • AI – Artificial Intelligence Systems: for control, optimization, and resilience across domains
